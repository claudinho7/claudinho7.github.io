About Me

Gamer. Sports Enthusiast. Meme Connoisseur. Music Addict. Nature Nomad
Creatve. Problem Solver. Versatile. Collaborative. Proactive. Reliable. Resilient

  • I am a passionate and creative game designer with a strong dedication to crafting immersive and engaging player experiences. With a background in Sales and HR prior to (and while) completing my Games Design and Production course, I have honed my problem solving, collaborative and leadership skills. My love for games and interactive media fuels my drive to push boundaries and create memorable experiences that captivate players.
  • I thrive on the excitement of game design, constantly seeking new ways to challenge myself and evolve as a designer. From brainstorming innovative ideas to meticulously refining gameplay mechanics, I am committed to delivering experiences that resonate with players on emotional and intellectual levels. I am particularly passionate about MMOs and multiplayer combat mechanics such as boss fights or weapon functionality, drawing inspiration from games like World of Warcraft or Dark Souls.
  • Recognizing the collaborative nature of game development, I excel in cross-functional teams, effectively communicating and collaborating with artists, programmers, and other stakeholders to bring ideas to life. My adaptability and versatility allow me to contribute to various aspects of the design process, whether it's level design, game mechanics, prototyping, or user experience.
  • In my portfolio, you will find a showcase of my diverse projects, including group or solo university projects or game jam porjects (no professional work currently). These projects demonstrate my ability to tackle design challenges, think critically, and deliver as high-quality work as i possibly can. I approach each project with a user-centric mindset, ensuring that players have meaningful and immersive experiences while keeping the entertainment high and frustation low.
  • I am always eager to connect with fellow game enthusiasts, industry professionals, and potential collaborators. If you are interested in discussing new projects, sharing ideas, or exploring opportunities, feel free to reach out to me. Let's collaborate and shape the future of gaming together.
