  • This is a custom level that uses the existing in-game assets to ensure the mod visually integrates seamlessly with the world of Kingdom Come: Deliverance 2.   • Through this project, I am demonstrating my ability to work within an existing framework and toolset to deliver meaningful new content, showcasing skills in technical design, systems implementation, and creative problem-solving while working with a limited documentation or support.
